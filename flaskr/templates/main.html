Super Metroid World Randomizer

Head to the Randomize! page to generate a ROM.

Randomizes the doors in Super Metroid with logic to ensure completability. In contrast with previous door randomizers, doors always take you back to where you came from.

If you get stuck, you can press Start+Select to warp back to Samus' Ship.

The randomizer is still in development. If you encounter any bugs in the game or problems with the logic, feel free to open an issue on the Randomizer github, and I will try to fix it. If you encounter any problems with the website, open an issue on the website github instead.
